My Favorite List

Food

I have a lot of foods that I like, but my absolute favorite is my mom's beef curry. As a kid, I wasn't a big fan of rice because it was a staple in our Asian household, but as I've grown up, I've come to appreciate my mom's cooking more than anything else. Her beef curry recipe is made with natural seasonings, such as masala, pepper, oil, and other spices, which give it a rich, complex flavor. The combination of spicy beef curry and rice is simply the best.
